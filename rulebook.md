Gaming Segment 

EFootball 


Rules & Regulations: 
●​ All Matches will be played via the Friendly Match mode. 
●​ Players are allowed to play with their own team. (Dream Team) 
●​ Every player must send a screenshot of the team they will be using 
before every match to the group created by the organizers. 
●​ The screenshot of the match result must be sent by both players to 
the group created by the organizers. 
●​ All matches will be knockout matches. 
●​ Players must comply with this rulebook and the game’s Terms of 
Service. 
●​ Use of emulators is strictly prohibited. All matches must be played 
on official, supported gaming platforms. Any player found using an 
emulator will be disqualified. 

No Mercy Rules: 
●​ If a player reaches a goal difference of 7 or more, they will be 
declared the winner. This rule does not apply in the Semi-Final and 
Final matches. 

●​ If a player fails to appear within 5 minutes of being called for their 
match,  
without a valid reason, a 3–0 walkover will be awarded to the 
opponent. 


Player Conduct & Game Bugs: 
●​ If a player displays offensive behavior, uses abusive language, or 
makes threats, they will be immediately disqualified and subject to 
strict disciplinary action. 

●​ If any interruption occurs in a match like network issue or 
something, must provide necessary proof and the game will be 
restarted with the same settings. 



Chess Online 


Rules & Regulations: 

●​ All matches will be played online on Lichess.org using the official 
tournament link. 

●​ Players of all ratings are allowed; no minimum games required on 
Lichess. 

●​ Only one Lichess account per player is allowed; multiple accounts 
are strictly prohibited. 

●​ Tournament format and time control will be announced by the 
organizers. 

●​ Match results recorded on Lichess will be considered final. 
●​ Use of chess engines, external assistance, or unfair means will lead 
to immediate disqualification. 
●​ Any offensive behavior or unsportsmanlike conduct will result in 
disqualification. 
●​ All players must join their matches on time when the match starts. 
●​ The top two players with the highest points will be declared the 
winners. 


PUBG 


Rules & Regulations: 
●​ This is a team-based event played in Team Deathmatch (TDM)  
●​ Each team must have 4 main players and may register 1 substitute 
player 
●​ Players must use their own PUBG Mobile accounts; use of 
emulators is not allowed. 
●​ Any form of hacking, cheating, scripting, or use of third-party tools 
will result in immediate disqualification 
●​ Teams must join the match on time; late entry may lead to a forfeit. 

●​ Players are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respectful 
behavior throughout the tournament. 

●​ The organizers’ decision is final in all cases and disputes.

Quiz Segment 
IT Olympiad 


Rules & Regulations: 

●​ This is an individual (solo) event. 
●​ Prior online registration is mandatory for all participants. 
●​ The competition will include 30 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ) 
based on IT knowledge and technology. 
●​ The time limit for the main quiz will be 20 minutes. 
●​ No unfair means (copying, AI tools, discussion with others) is 
allowed. 
●​ Any participant found violating rules will be disqualified. 
●​ Participation is restricted to currently enrolled BNMPC students 
only. 


Gaming Quiz 


Rules & Regulations: 

●​ This is a solo event — only one participant is allowed. 
●​ The quiz will consist of 30 MCQs based on Gaming knowledge. 
●​ The time limit for the main quiz will be 20 minutes. 
●​ In case of a tie, tie-breaker questions will be conducted with a time 
limit of 10 minutes. 
●​ The Quiz Master’s decision is final and binding in all situations. 
●​ Participation is restricted to currently enrolled BNMPC students 
only.

Signature Segment 
Crack The Code 


Rules & Regulations: 

●​ The competition will be conducted online on HackerRank. 
●​ Participants must solve all problems within the stipulated time. 
●​ Submissions made after the time limit will not be considered. 
●​ Use of unauthorized resources, external help, or collaboration is 
strictly prohibited.

Sci-Fi Story Writing 


Rules & Regulations: 
●​ Open to all registered participants of the event. 
●​  Individual participation only (no teams). 
●​ The story must be science-fiction based (future, technology, AI, 
space, time travel, etc.). 
●​ Any content copied from movies, books, or the internet will lead to 
disqualification. 
●​ AI-generated stories are not allowed. Any detected AI content will 
result in disqualification. 
●​ The story can be written in Bangla or English. 
●​ The story must be submitted within the given time. Late submissions 
will not be accepted. 
●​ Participation is restricted to currently enrolled BNMPC students 
only. 


Submission: 

●​ Participants have to submit their story in PDF or DOCX format. 
●​ Stories must be submitted through the Technobit website or the 
official submission link sent via email too.
